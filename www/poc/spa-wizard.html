Welcome to Boorker Watchdog

Let's get your monitoring system ready. This wizard will guide you through the essential setup in about 2 minutes.

What is Boorker Watchdog?

A distributed sensor monitoring platform for your home or workspace. It watches for temperature changes, vibrations, water leaks, and more — alerting you when something needs attention.

What we'll set up

Device identity, WiFi connection, sensor detection, and alert preferences. You can always change these later in Settings.

Name Your Device

Give this node a memorable name and location so you can identify it easily.

Tip: Be specific

If you have multiple nodes, names like "Basement Unit" or "Garage Monitor" are easier to remember than "Node 1".

This appears in dashboards and alerts

Connect to WiFi

Select your network to enable dashboard access and remote monitoring.

Already connected?
